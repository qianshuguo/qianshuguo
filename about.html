About

Qianshu Guo is a Chinese music composer based in New York City. He specializes in crafting orchestral and hybrid scores for visual media, blending classical textures with modern electronic and jazz influences.

His music is known for its rich harmonic language and emotional storytelling. Trained in both classical composition and jazz keyboard, Qianshu brings a versatile voice to each project, drawing from his experience in orchestration, sound production, and cross-genre experimentation. His works span film, games, and multimedia collaborations.

With roots in China and professional experience in the U.S., Qianshu's musical voice reflects a cross-cultural perspective. From sweeping orchestral scores to groove-driven electronic soundscapes, his compositions aim to deepen narrative impact and evoke lasting emotion.

In addition to scoring, he is passionate about pop music production and occasionally writes songs, bringing a melodic sensibility and emotional immediacy into his media work.
